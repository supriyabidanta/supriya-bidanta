About

I’m a Ph.D. candidate in Bioengineering at Indiana University (CNS). My work integrates biology, mathematics and machine learning to analyze single-cell and spatial transcriptomics data, construct mechanistic and multiscale models, and develop computational tools for translational research in cancer, immunology and aging.

Notable techniques
  • Single-cell / spatial transcriptomics (scRNA-seq, Visium)
  • Deep learning (CNNs, GANs), GNNs
  • Multiscale modeling (PhysiCell, CompuCell3D)
Tools & infra

  • AWS, Docker, Git, Airflow
